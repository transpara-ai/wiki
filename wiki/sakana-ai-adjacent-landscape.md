# Sakana AI Adjacent Technologies and Organisations

**Sakana AI Adjacent Technologies and Organisations extends [[sakana-ai-evaluation|the first Sakana report]] outward into the research ecosystem around Sakana AI — collaborators, recursive self-improvement work, autonomous research systems, evolutionary AI tooling, and collective-intelligence orchestration — carried on an adversarial deep-research workflow that keeps its refuted leads visible.**

## What Changed with the Research

The report (TAI-RES-2026-002, most recently v1.0.1 — a classification correction
for the record) maps Sakana AI as sitting at the intersection of:

- evolutionary computation;
- autonomous research systems;
- recursive self-improving coding agents;
- multi-agent and multi-model orchestration;
- nature-inspired AI tooling.

The closest named research collaborator is Jeff Clune's lab at UBC / Vector
Institute, tied to both the Darwin Gödel Machine and the AI Scientist line.

## The Boundary

This is a companion research map, not a doctrine change. It does not make Sakana
AI, Jeff Clune's lab, EvoJAX, EvoScientist, or Together AI part of the
Civilization architecture. It identifies external systems that may later be
evaluated through a governed research-to-doctrine path.
