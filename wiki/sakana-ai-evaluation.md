# Sakana AI Capability Evaluation

**Sakana AI Capability Evaluation** is the first released internal Sakana
research artifact in the Stage 0 external-landscape corpus. It compares Sakana
AI's model, algorithm, and autonomous-research work against the
[[the-civilization|Transpara-AI Civilization]] architecture.

## Placement

The Stage 0 placement index puts this report under Civilization
external-landscape research, not under Transpara LLC Platform competitive
intelligence. The reason is that the report asks what the Civilization can learn
from Sakana's self-improving AI, multi-model orchestration, and autonomous
research patterns.

It is research evidence. It is not accepted doctrine, not a product-positioning
document, and not authority to change customer/demo access policy.

## Central Finding

The report finds Sakana AI and the Civilization complementary rather than
directly comparable:

| Layer | Sakana AI | Transpara-AI Civilization |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Primary locus | model and algorithm layer | governance, substrate, and societal layer |
| Self-improvement | DGM, AI Scientist, ShinkaEvolve, recursive improvement research | EventGraph accountability, authority, review, and governed capability evolution |
| Orchestration | TRINITY, Conductor, AB-MCTS, productized multi-model coordination | Hive roles, Work DAG, EventGraph causal records, AuthorityDecision paths |
| Deepest advantage | active publication/product research loop | causal accountability, agent rights, and self-sustaining economy |

## Research Themes

The report identifies Sakana as active in:

- recursive self-improvement through the Darwin Godel Machine and related RSI
  work;
- automated research loops through AI Scientist;
- multi-model orchestration through TRINITY, Conductor, and AB-MCTS;
- commercial products including Fugu, Fugu Ultra, Marlin, and Sakana Chat;
- finance, defense, and intelligence vertical expansion.

## Learning Opportunities

The six learning opportunities named by the report are:

| Opportunity | Civilization implication |
| --- | --- |
| Darwin Godel Machine | strengthen self-modification gates with test-verified improvement thresholds |
| AB-MCTS | evolve Allocator model choice from static tiers to outcome-based selection |
| AI Scientist | expand the Researcher role into a full discovery loop |
| Digital Red Queen | formalize adversarial agent-pair workflows |
| TRINITY / Conductor | measure and evolve collaboration patterns |
| Sakana Marlin | treat long-running autonomous research sessions as proof-of-market for scheduled self-directing work |

## Boundary

The report does not promote Sakana AI into the Civilization control plane. It
does not make Sakana a kernel, truth source, Work replacement, release authority,
capability promoter, certification authority, or factory controller.
